# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 1. Project Title / Headline

📉 **Customer Churn Insights: Interactive Customer Retention Analysis Dashboard**

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze customer churn patterns, identify key factors influencing customer retention, and help businesses understand customer behavior. The dashboard provides actionable insights through dynamic visualizations and KPIs to support data-driven decision-making.

# 2. Short Description / Purpose

The **Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI report created to analyze customer churn behavior and identify trends affecting customer retention.

The dashboard enables businesses to explore customer data, understand churn patterns, and detect high-risk customer segments that may leave the service.

This dashboard can be used by **business analysts, customer success teams, marketing departments, and decision-makers** to improve customer retention strategies and reduce churn rates.

# 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* **📊 Power BI Desktop** – Primary platform used to design and develop the interactive dashboard
* **📂 Power Query** – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation
* **🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used to create calculated measures and KPIs such as churn rate and customer segmentation
* **📊 Data Modeling** – Relationships between customer, service, and demographic datasets were created for deeper analysis
* **📁 File Format** – `.pbix` used for dashboard development and visualization design

# 4. Data Source

The dataset used in this project contains **customer subscription and demographic data used to analyze churn behavior**.

The dataset includes information such as:

* Customer demographics (age group, gender, location)
* Subscription plans and service types
* Customer tenure and usage patterns
* Monthly charges and total charges
* Customer churn status (Stayed or Churned)

The dataset was cleaned and transformed using **Power Query** before being analyzed and visualized in Power BI.

# 5. Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem

Customer churn is a major challenge for many businesses, especially in industries such as telecommunications, subscription services, and SaaS platforms. Losing customers directly impacts revenue and growth.

Companies collect large amounts of customer data, but identifying **why customers leave** and **which customers are at risk of churning** can be difficult without proper analysis.

Key questions addressed in this dashboard include:

• What percentage of customers are leaving the service?
• Which customer segments have the highest churn rates?
• What factors influence customer churn the most?
• Which services or plans are associated with higher churn?

# Goal of the Dashboard

To build an interactive analytics dashboard that:

• Helps businesses identify **customer churn patterns**
• Highlights **high-risk customer segments**
• Enables exploration of customer demographics and service usage
• Supports **data-driven retention strategies**

# Walkthrough of Key Visuals

### Key KPIs (Top Section)

Displays important metrics such as:

* Total Customers
* Total Churned Customers
* Customer Retention Rate
* Churn Rate Percentage

These KPIs provide a quick overview of customer retention performance.

### Customer Churn Distribution (Donut Chart)

A donut chart visualizing the proportion of **customers who stayed vs customers who churned**, giving a quick snapshot of overall churn performance.

### Churn by Demographics (Bar Chart)

A bar chart comparing churn rates across demographic categories such as:

* Gender
* Age groups
* Customer segments

This helps identify which groups are more likely to leave the service.

### Monthly Charges vs Churn (Column/Bar Chart)

A visual comparison showing how **monthly charges influence customer churn**, helping analysts understand whether pricing impacts customer retention.

### Customer Tenure Analysis (Area Chart)

An area chart displaying churn trends based on **customer tenure**, helping businesses understand whether newer or long-term customers are more likely to churn.

### Interactive Filters (Slicers)

Interactive slicers allow users to dynamically filter the dashboard by:

* Gender
* Contract type
* Internet service type
* Payment method
* Customer tenure group

These filters enable deeper exploration of churn patterns across different customer segments.
